idea: trading game

  • each person you meet has something they want, and something they'll give you in return
  • eg. "I'll swap 2 pizzas for 1 cat"
  • you get points every time you make a trade, and they get progressively more difficult.
  • at first you're just carrying the inventory of a single trade, but later on you have to make a few smaller trades in order to make the bigger one.
